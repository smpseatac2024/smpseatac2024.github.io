FAQ

Scalp Micropigmentation or SMP is a specialized form of cosmetic tattoo. The SMP impressions give the appearance of short hair stubbles. Achieving the look of a full head of hair after a close buzz or shave!

It varies from customer to customer. Everyone's pain tolerance is different and pain levels can range from 0 to 6. Various parts of the head can have a different pain threshold. The majority of my clients fall asleep during the process.

The SMP session typically takes 4 or more sessions. We space the sessions a week apart to ensure the scalp has time to heal for the next session

An estimate of up to 4 to 5 years, it can get lighter and lighter by the year. This can be managed with a touchup session every 2 to 3 years.

Its best to avoid extended sun exposure, shaving, soaking it in sweat, and anything that would graze or cause friction to the treated area for a week after each session. For optimal SMP retention, its recommended to follow a simple aftercare routine 1-week post-session.
